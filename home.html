What is ChanAcademy?

A while back, my co-founders and I had a vision for the parkour community. We were disappointed by the lack of free educational resources for students of parkour and we knew we needed to we find a solution. A couple of coffee-fueled long nights and traceur meetups later, ChanAcademy was born.

ChanAcademy is the Internet's hub for parkour instruction. With a focus on safety, fundamentals, and personal improvement, our goal is to make parkour the world's most beloved sport. Using our platform's tiered educational tracks geared for novices, professionals, and everyone in between, you won't ever be at a loss for training and finding your next biggest challenge.

Join ChanAcademy today and help us create the next generation of traceurs!
